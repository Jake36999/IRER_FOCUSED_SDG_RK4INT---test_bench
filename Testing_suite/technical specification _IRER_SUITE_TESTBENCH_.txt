For "Red Tier" files—those exhibiting extreme complexity or high-risk keywords—the scanner orchestrates a 3-Round AI Analysis pipeline using local LLMs (typically deepseek-r1-0528-qwen3-8b via LM Studio).
The 3-Round Pipeline & Stability Warnings
1. Round 1: Component Analysis (450 tokens): A granular audit of specific high-risk functions.
2. Round 2: Overview Consolidation (3748+ tokens): Synthesis of all Round 1 findings into an architectural summary. Note: Experience in v4.5 shows Round 2 is prone to client disconnects due to context size; the system now handles these interruptions gracefully, allowing for automatic retries.
3. Round 3: Next Steps (279 tokens): Generation of prioritized remediation tasks.
Critical Implementation Detail: Data Sync
A major bug fix in v4.5 corrected a "Data Field Mismatch" where the system checked chunk_data.get("files") while chunks only contained a data field. The production-ready logic now loads a fresh analysis state from files/{file_id}.json to ensure the AI is operating on the most recent AST and entropy results.
